Our Story

"Lusaka deserved a rooftop kitchen that took Indo-Chinese and Pan-Asian cooking seriously. So we built one."

Lantern & Wok opened in 2019 on the top floor of a converted warehouse in Rhodes Park. Every dish leaves an open wok station you can watch from the bar.

We import chilli oil, char siu, and five-spice from Guangzhou, and pair them with vegetables sourced daily from Soweto Market. The dining room seats 120, the rooftop bar another 60 — and the sunset is the same every night, which is exactly why guests keep returning for it.
