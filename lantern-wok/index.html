Our Story

A kitchen built around the wok, and a room built around the view.

Lantern & Wok opened in 2019 on the top floor of a converted warehouse in Rhodes Park, with one idea: Lusaka deserved a rooftop kitchen that took Indo-Chinese and Pan-Asian cooking seriously.

Every dish leaves an open wok station you can watch from the bar. We import our chilli oil, char siu, and five-spice from Guangzhou, and pair them with vegetables sourced daily from Soweto Market.

The dining room seats 120, the rooftop bar seats another 60, and the sunset over Lusaka's northern suburbs is the same every night — which is exactly why people keep coming back for it.
