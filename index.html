What we do

Our programs

Three core pillars guide our work in communities across Lusaka.

Education
School Support Fund

Covering school fees, uniforms, and learning materials for vulnerable children to keep them in the classroom.

Skills Training
Digital & Vocational Skills

Hands-on training in computing, trades, and entrepreneurship to help young people build sustainable livelihoods.

Mentorship
Peer & Adult Mentorship

Connecting youth with mentors who guide them through education, career, and personal development.
