About

Who I Am

I'm Cletus, a full-stack web developer based in Lusaka, Zambia. I'm currently studying for a Diploma in Computer Networks and Data Communication at Evelyn Hone College of Applied Arts and Commerce, and I've been building real, live web applications alongside my studies.

I specialize in the Flask + React + PostgreSQL stack — from designing the database, building the API, to deploying the final product on Railway and Vercel. I've worked on marketplaces, student portals, AI tools, and business sites.

My focus is on building things that actually work for Zambian businesses — practical, affordable, and built to last. I take on projects of all sizes, from a simple one-page site to a full web platform.

I also completed an industrial attachment at Livingstone City Council's ICT Department, where I handled IT support, server maintenance, and staff training.
